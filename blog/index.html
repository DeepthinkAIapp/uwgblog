Welcome to Utah Water Gardens

We're Utah's premier water garden specialists, dedicated to creating and maintaining stunning ponds and water features that thrive in our unique climate. With years of experience serving homeowners, parks and recreation departments, golf courses, and commercial properties across the state, we understand the challenges and opportunities that Utah's diverse landscape presents.

Whether you're dreaming of a peaceful koi pond, a dramatic waterfall, or a complete water garden transformation, our team of experts is here to bring your vision to life. Why Choose Utah Water Gardens?

Our team consists of certified water garden professionals with deep knowledge of Utah's climate, soil conditions, and local regulations. We've successfully completed hundreds of water garden projects across the state, from small backyard ponds to large commercial installations, municipal parks, and golf course water features.
